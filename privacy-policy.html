Air Sky Travels - Privacy Policy

Welcome to Air Sky Travels, your trusted platform for seamless airline bookings. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By accessing our website and utilizing our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect:

1.1. Personal Information:

  • We collect information such as your name, contact details, and payment information when you make a booking.

1.2. Booking Information:

  • Details related to your flight bookings, including travel dates, destinations, and preferences.

1.3. Device and Usage Information:

  • Information about the device you use to access our website, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

1.4. Cookies:

  • We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and gather information about your preferences and activities on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information:

2.1. Booking and Services:

  • Your information is used to process and confirm your flight bookings and provide the services you requested.

2.2. Communication:

  • We may use your contact information to communicate with you regarding your bookings, updates, and promotional offers.

2.3. Improvement of Services:

  • We analyze user data to enhance and personalize our services, improve website functionality, and tailor our offerings to your preferences.

2.4. Legal Compliance:

  • We may use your information to comply with legal obligations or respond to legal requests.

3. How We Share Your Information:

3.1. Airlines and Service Providers:

  • We share necessary information with airlines and service providers to fulfill your booking requests.

3.2. Legal Requirements:

  • We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or in response to a legal request.

3.3. Third-Party Partners:

  • With your consent, we may share information with third-party partners for marketing or promotional purposes.

4. Security Measures:

4.1. Data Security:

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4.2. Payment Security:

  • Your payment information is encrypted and securely processed through trusted payment gateways.

5. Your Choices and Rights:

5.1. Access and Correction:

  • You have the right to access and correct your personal information. Contact us at privacy@airskytravels.com for assistance.

5.2. Opt-Out:

  • You can opt-out of marketing communications by following the instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

6. Children's Privacy:

6.1. Our services are not directed towards individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us to request its deletion.

7. Updates to the Privacy Policy:

7.1. We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

8. Contact Information:

8.1. For any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy, please contact us at privacy@airskytravels.com.
